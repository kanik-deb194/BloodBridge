Why BloodBridge

Built for speed.
Designed to save lives.

A unified platform bridging every part of the blood supply chain — from donor to patient — in real time.

🩸

Real-Time Blood Tracking

Monitor blood unit availability across all registered banks and hospitals with live inventory updates every 60 seconds.

🚨

Emergency Alerts

Instantly broadcast urgent blood needs to nearby verified donors and partner facilities via SMS, app, and email.

📍

Location-Based Matching

Smart geo-matching connects the closest compatible donors to recipients within minutes, not hours.

🏥

Hospital Integration

Seamless API connectivity with hospital EMR systems for automated blood requisition and real-time cross-matching.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive insights on donation trends, regional shortages, and supply forecasting for better health policy.

🔐

Secure & Verified

All donors and facilities are identity-verified. Blood units are digitally tracked end-to-end with full chain of custody.

Process

How it works

Three simple steps to connect life-saving blood with those who need it.

01

Register & Verify

Donors, hospitals, and blood banks register with full identity and medical verification for a trusted network.

02

Request or Offer

Hospitals post blood requests instantly. Donors receive geo-targeted alerts for compatible types nearby.

03

Bridge the Gap

BloodBridge orchestrates logistics — matching, routing, and confirming delivery from donor to patient.
